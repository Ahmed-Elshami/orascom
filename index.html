Who We Are

Orascom Services is the holding company of Orascom Trading S.A.E., Orascom Free Zone, and National Equipment Company. Orascom Services is a prominent player in fields of commercial agencies, equipment importation and distribution, genset packaging, telecom services, and equipment rental. We provide unparalleled sales and after sales services with a commitment to excellence in every aspect of our operations. Established over four decades ago by the late Eng. Onsi Sawiris, Orascom Services represents a diverse range of renowned Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) in Egypt. Our extensive portfolio serve multiple industries, including construction, infrastructure, industrial manufacturing, telecom services and agriculture
